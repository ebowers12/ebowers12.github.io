May I pick up my order?

Yes please! Using coupon code “PICKUP” at checkout will apply a discount and let me know that you’d like to pick up. I’ll be in touch as soon as your order is ready to arrange a time. All pick ups are no-contact, your order will be packed and waiting on my front porch in Tacoma’s Lincoln District at a time that works best for you.

How about Custom Orders?

I have Custom Home Utensil Holders and Spoon Rests available for order in my shop. (see photos in the gallery) I am also always adding new cities and towns to the Skyline Series, with priority to larger cities, state capitals, and locations in states and regions where I have not yet done a lot of work. Let me know what you are looking for and I’ll tell you what I can do!

Are you adding new wholesale accounts?

Not at this time. I discontinued all wholesale and consignment in 2020 when retail orders in my online shop grew into a full time job. Thank you for your interest, and for SUPPORTING HANDMADE!
