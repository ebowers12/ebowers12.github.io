Some info about how this program works:
When someone enters the program, they are assigned a specialist and other people who are striving to place them into permanent housing.
The basic idea of this approach is that people who are currently experiencing homelessness need basic needs such as food and a place to sleep
before they can begin to think about things like getting a job.
