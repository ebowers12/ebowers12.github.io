The implementation of La Quinta has been proven to be a very controversial topic.

The following video provides a little more insight into the pro side of the argument, of which the City of Kirkland follows.

Arguments Against the Implementation of La Quinta:

The arguments against La Quinta mainly involve the safety of the surrounding area - including schools and office buildings. This is because people are still allowed to drink and use drugs while in the center, which parents don't want around their kids especially.

safety: kids

Arguments For the Implementation of La Quinta:

The arguments for La Quinta revolve around the safety of the greater good as providing a means of shelter for those who really need it would help their safety and quality of life. The location of La Quinta is perfect as well as it is very accessible due to its proximity to the park and ride.

safety: greater good
